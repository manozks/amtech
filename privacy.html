Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2024

At AMTECHSYSTEMS Systems, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address when you contact us for consultations or information about our services. We also collect information about your interactions with our website, including your IP address and browsing behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our hospitality technology consulting services.
  • Communicate with you regarding your inquiries and projects.
  • Analyze website usage to improve our online presence.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information to trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@amtechsystems.com.au.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
