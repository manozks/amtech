Enterprise Oracle Expertise, Personalised Service

AMTECHSYSTEMS was founded by Ex Oracle Consultants and Technical Delivery leaders, collectivey 40+ years at Oracle Corporation across hospitality and enterprise technology.

Delivering some of the largest Oracle Simphony deployments in the APAC region — including 600+ workstation casino rollouts, 3,000+ terminal food & beverage chains, and restaurant groups spanning 30+ countries — we brings a rare combination of deep technical expertise and real-world operational experience to every project.
