About AM Tech Systems

Enterprise Oracle Expertise, Personalised Service

AM Tech Systems was founded by Aaron McGowan, a Senior Oracle Implementation Consultant and Technical Lead with over 10 years at Oracle Corporation and 15+ years across hospitality and enterprise technology.

Having delivered some of the largest Oracle Simphony deployments in the APAC region — including 600+ workstation casino rollouts, 3,000+ terminal food & beverage chains, and restaurant groups spanning 30+ countries — Aaron brings a rare combination of deep technical expertise and real-world operational experience to every project.
